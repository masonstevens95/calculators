Methodology — All values from PWA_PUBLIC_VIEW_4-30-2026.accdb (Forsyth County). 99,369 parcels filtered to CITY containing "WINSTON-SALEM", weighted by CITY_PCNT for boundary-straddling parcels.
Target — replaces only the real-property portion of W-S revenue ($193.05M). Personal property tax stays at today's $0.5266/$100 (≈$30.6M, untouched).
Math — at shift s ∈ [0, 1]: improvement rate = (1−s) × $0.5266; land rate = (1−s) × $0.5266 + s × pure-LVT-rate. Linear interpolation keeps total real-property revenue constant.
Caveat — North Carolina Constitution Article V §2(2) requires uniform taxation, so split-rate LVT requires General Assembly enabling legislation. See Land Value Tax - Winston-Salem Proposal.md.
